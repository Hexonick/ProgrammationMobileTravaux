Elementary miracle cast by clerics. Restores HP.

Common miracle among cleric knights. Create shockwave.

AoE effect that does no damage but knocks back most surrounding enemies.

Notes

  1. Must be very close to cause knockdown.
  2. Can also deflect sorceries such as Homing Soulmass, as well as arrows and crossbow bolts.
  3. One cast will not knock down enemies with high poise.

Miracle of Bishop Havel the Rock. Cover body in defensive magic coating.

Usage

  1. Reduces incoming magic damage for a short duration.

Miracle passed down to those bound by the Warrior of Sunlight covenant. Hurl lightning spear.

Notes

At 50 faith, using sunlight talisman and ring of the sun's firstborn, the three lightning spear miracles does the following damage on a random undead in the undead burg:

  1. Lightning spear - 458
  2. Great lightning spear - 644
  3. Sunlight spear - 861

Common miracle amongst cleric knights. Gradual HP restoration.

Notes

  1. The Ring of the Sun Princess will increase the healing to 12HP/s (duration unchanged), for a total of 720HP.

Miracle wielded by Lord Gwyn's firstborn. Boost right weapon with rays of Sun.

Notes

  1. Sunlight Blade scales with your Talisman's Magic Adjust stat. It does not scale with your Faith stat. Be sure to wield the best talisman for your Faith level in order to get the maximum benefits of this miracle.

Ancient sorcery of the lost land of Oolacile. Cast a bright light upon surroundings.

Notes

  1. You are only able to see your own light, meaning you cannot see the light of a co-op player, or vice versa.
  2. This spell is especially useful in the Tomb of Giants.
  3. Can be casted while any other buff is active, can even override itself.

Sorcery developed by Big Hat Logan: Homing soulmass. Life originates in the soul; no wonder the soulmass draws toward it.

Notes

  1. The number of orbs appearing are dependent on the Intelligence level: 18=2, 19=3, 25=4, 31=5.
  2. Can be successfully cast without an initial "lock on". Multiple soul arrows will hover above the player for 100 seconds. Each individual soul arrow will seek the closest target available in the direction the player is facing, within close to medium range.
  3. Projectiles will dissipate if they come into direct contact with an object, i.e. wall, bush, etc.

Elementary sorcery. Fire a soul arrow.

Notes

  1. Starting spell for the Sorcerer class.
  2. This spell seems to deal damage equal to (Catalyst Mag. Adj. x 0.9). This was tested by using two catalysts at Int 50 against the first Undead in Firelink Shrine.

Sorcery for casters who wield swords. Magic augmentation: left shield.

Notes

  1. Blocked Magic attacks' damage are reduced by an extra 30% (maximum magic resistance being 90%).
  2. Unequipping the shield, or letting the spell run out, cancels this effect.
  3. Does not raise damage when used on weapons on the left hand.

Sorcery developed by Big Hat Logan. Fire piercing soul spear.

Notes

  1. Goes through enemies, allowing you to hit a line of foes.
  2. Does heavy stamina damage if blocked, staggering most opponents.
  3. Arcs slightly towards opponents.

Sorcery developed by Logan during his infatuation with Seath the Scaleless. Emit crystal breath of Seath the Scaleless.

Notes

  1. Can only hit targets on the ground.
  2. Can hit multiple targets.
  3. A slow casting time comparable to heavy soul arrow.
  4. This spell seems to deal damage equal to (Catalyst Mag. Adj. x 2.5). This was tested by using two catalysts at Int 50 against the first Undead in Firelink Shrine.

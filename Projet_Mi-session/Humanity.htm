Rare tiny black sprite found on corpses. Use to gain 1 humanity and restore a large amount of HP.

Usage

  1. Increases Humanity count by 1 when consumed.
  2. Restores full health when consumed. (at least 2000+)
  3. Can be traded to Snuggly for a Ring of Sacrifice.
  4. Can be sold to Kingseeker Frampt for 1,000 souls.

Prix: 50$




Rare tiny black sprite found on corpses. Very unusual twin humanity. Use to gain 2 humanity and restore a large amount of HP.

Usage

  1. Increases Humanity count by 2 when consumed.
  2. Restores a large amount of health when consumed. (how much?)
  3. Can be traded to Snuggly for a Ring of Sacrifice.
  4. Can be sold to Kingseeker Frampt for 2,000 souls.

Prix: 80$
